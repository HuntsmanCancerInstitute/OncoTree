COMMON LLM ERRORS TO AVOID:

2) There is no oncotree_tissue_code that includes the word esophageal or esophagus.  These are always classified as STOMACH.
3) Do not return an "oncotree_tissue_code" that isn't present in the catalog. Double check before responding against the list in the RESPONSE FORMAT in JSON section above.
